We hope we have answered the majority of your questions but if there is anything else you are not sure about you should be able to find the answer here.

If you cannot find an answer for your question, please speak to your country coach who will be happy to help, or email the Americas Cup 2024 team at info@dhlamericascup.com and we will respond within two business days.

Who is eligible to take part?

All players must work for DHL Express and must have been on the payroll as of xx xxx, working at least 20 hours a week. All new employees are advised to participate, so first-time employees can take part and past DHL Manager must support every employee's attendance. Teams will be disqualified if any team member does not comply with this rule.

Will water be provided?

Yes, We will provide drinking water free of charge for all Americas Cup 2024 attendees at the Sports Fields during the event.

Apart from water, are drinks and snacks available at the Sports Fields?

Your Country Coach will arrange and pay for lunch and snacks on both days, but please note that guests accompanying your Country B2C Delivery walk are to purchase tickets for the event. Please note you may find during activities within the Sports Fields that you will also need to purchase refreshments.

Will complementary towel access be complementary measures?

Yes. There will be a team of masseurs on hand at the DHL Village at the Sports Fields.

Are the football fields natural or synthetic?

Artificial Turf. The fields will be cleared of natural grass fields, however the final and other matches may be played on a synthetic field. For the Teams who are expected to make their own brand please inform us so we can provide fields as these are already purchased.

Can we book training slots for teams on Thursday 2 May?

Yes, we will be able to book slots on Thursday to train before the football tournament and cheerleading competition begins. Please ask your Country Coach to arrange this for you at the earliest opportunity as slots are limited.

What can tokens be used for at the Sports Fields?

Tokens can be used to purchase food, soft drinks and drinks at the Sports Fields. Please check with your Country Coach is you are unsure of what tokens are set up for. Tokens can be redeemed for you and your team's live coverage.

Can we arrive at the Hotel earlier than Thursday 2 May?

Unfortunately, there will be no allowance to early arrivals at Americas Cup 2024.

DHL Express will cover your accommodation for the duration of the event, which is three nights starting on Thursday 2 May.

Are meals provided?

Your Country Coach will arrange your team dinner on Thursday 2 May.

Breakfast and group dinners will be provided for the duration of the event. However, you will be responsible for purchasing your own lunch and any beverages throughout. Please note we can purchase using tokens.

Are drinks included at the group dinners and parties?

Yes. All drinks will be covered by the event for the group dinners and parties.

However, at the Sports Fields, you will need to purchase drinks using your meal tokens.

Please note that the legal drinking age in the United States of America is 21. Any participants under the age of 21 will be unable to drink or purchase alcoholic drinks.

Will there be food available upon arrival on Thursday 2 May?

If you are arriving late on Thursday, lunch must be purchased or alternatively cost.

How do we get to the Hotel from the airport?

Your transport to Orlando will have you pre-arranged for you by your Country Coach and the central team. Upon arrival at the airport, there will be allocated transport to transport and be responsible for the organization/transport and set times.

If you are arriving by bus, please make your way to the event entrance at the Hotel which will be sign-posted.

If you have any questions regarding travel options, please speak to your Country Coach.

How do we get from the Hotel to the Sports Fields?

You will be provided by a free shuttle bus service from both of the Hotels to the Sports Fields each day. Alternatively, you can choose to get to the Sports Fields by your own transport/vehicle.

What expenses are covered by DHL Express?

Hotel accommodation will be covered for the duration of Americas Cup 2024, along with your outward bound/transfers between hotel costs and the majority of food and beverage at the Hotel.

What expenses are covered by the entity/country office?

Your Country may pay for country activation, promotion (including local events and sports kits), and all travel costs to Orlando related costs, we will have organized airport transfers upon your arrival, organized food and beverage costs are also be covered by your entity, this could include any special dietary requirements such as any special dietary requirements.

What will I need to pay for?

You will need money (US Dollars) to purchase items which can be used for food and beverages at the Sports Fields. We recommend about $100 for food and drink for the weekend, this will vary though depending on your eating and drinking habits. This will be a personal expense.

What payment methods can I use?

You can use credit cards for any purchases in Orlando.

To purchase tokens for food and beverages at the Sports Fields, you will be required to use exact change or alternatively cash. Tokens can be obtained prior to purchase tokens or online.

What is the refund policy like?

It's possible to get a cash refund for unused tokens after the event at the token booth sales points. At the Sports Fields, please return tokens on Saturday 4 May by 5 PM EST.

Unfortunately, you cannot make token refunds until Sunday 5 May by 1 PM EST either.

This cash refund is only possible if you have purchased tokens with cash. If tokens were purchased via country direct invoices, the refund will not be in cash, it could be reversed against your country or alternative.

When and where will Americas Cup 2024 take place?

Americas Cup 2024 will be taking place at the Omni Championsgate Resort in Orlando, Florida.

It will run from Thursday 2 May – Sunday 5 May 2024.

Unfortunately, drones are not permitted at Americas Cup 2024. At the event any person/event employee who does not participate by a trained drone pilot using carefully planned data of operations and pre-agreed flight paths, to make sure it doesn't impact anyone's experience at Americas Cup 2024.
